## Configuration System Overview

### Two-Phase Input System

**Phase 1: Constraints** 🏪
Define the unchangeable parameters of your retail environment:
- Physical space limitations
- Safety and compliance requirements
- Operational boundaries
- Brand standards

**Phase 2: Configuration** ⚙️
Set your strategic goals and tuning parameters:
- Business objectives
- Performance targets
- Anchor-and-Spokes optimization settings
- Implementation preferences

### Processing Flow
The system processes both inputs to generate customized artifacts that respect your constraints while optimizing for your strategic goals.

### Generated Artifacts 📋
- **Anchor & Spoke Map**: Visual layout with precise product positioning
- **Product Relationship Cards**: Detailed merchandising instructions
- **Validation Checklist**: Quality assurance framework
- **Execution Timeline**: Implementation roadmap

### Configuration Templates

Copy and customize these templates:
- `constraints.yaml.template` → `constraints.yaml`
- `config.yaml.template` → `config.yaml`

The system uses placeholder replacement to personalize all artifacts to your specific retail environment and business objectives.
