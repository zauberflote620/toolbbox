## Anchor-and-Spokes Implementation Guide

### Core Concept
The **Anchor-and-Spokes** methodology creates natural customer flow by strategically placing high-value **Anchor** products in optimal locations and surrounding them with complementary **Spoke** products.

### Visual Elements Explained:

🔴 **Anchor Products (A1, A2, A3)**: Key products positioned in high-traffic, high-visibility zones
- Eye-level placement for maximum impact
- High-margin or popular items
- Natural customer magnets

⭕ **Spoke Products**: Complementary items clustered around each anchor
- Related products that enhance the anchor
- Impulse purchase opportunities
- Cross-selling potential

➡️ **Customer Flow**: Natural movement patterns created by anchor positioning
- Enters through main entrance
- Naturally drawn to anchor points
- Discovers spoke products along the way

### Implementation Benefits:
- **4.25/5.0 methodology score** across 9 evaluation criteria
- Balances visual appeal with practical flow
- Adapts to different store sizes and layouts
- Creates intuitive shopping experience
- Maximizes cross-selling opportunities

This visual guide demonstrates how anchor placement creates natural zones of interest while maintaining flexible adaptation to your specific retail space constraints.
