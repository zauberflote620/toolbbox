How to Play

  • Goal: Match artworks to visitor preferences
  • Choose wisely: You have 3 artworks to pick from each round
  • Drag your chosen artwork onto the display wall
  • Watch the visitor's reaction (green = happy, yellow = neutral, red = unhappy)
  • Match both theme AND style for perfect matches (stars)
  • Goal: Earn 15 stars to complete the level!
